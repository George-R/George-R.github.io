apr, 2017 | d3.js

Exploring triple-doubles efficiency with TDR

An original data-driven analysis of the history of triple-doubles in the NBA. For this, the new metric TDR, or Triple-double rating, was created to measure the quality of a players' triple-double games. Interactive D3.js charts and tables were developed to allow users to explore the data and have a deeper understanding of what efficiency in this context means. Developed with partners Brooks Sutherland and Felippe Rodrigues. Published in Brazil (Portugese).
